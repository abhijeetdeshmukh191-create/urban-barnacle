🚨 Samuel Trading Company – AML Fraud Detection & Mule Account Investigation Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This Power BI project was developed to analyze suspicious financial transactions, identify potential money laundering activities, and support fraud investigations through interactive visualizations.

The dashboard provides investigators and compliance teams with actionable insights into:

Suspicious transaction patterns
High-risk entities
Mule account detection
Geographic concentration of suspicious activities
Fraud trend monitoring
Investigation support and decision-making
🎯 Business Objective

Financial institutions face increasing risks from:

Money Laundering
Mule Account Networks
Fraudulent Transactions
Suspicious Customer Behavior

This dashboard helps investigators quickly identify:

✅ High-risk customers

✅ Potential mule accounts

✅ Fraud concentration areas

✅ Transaction anomalies

✅ Investigation priorities
